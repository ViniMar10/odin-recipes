Banana almond protein smoothie

Description:

This is a delicious smoothie and makes for a mean-postworkout shake too. It delivers 21g of proteins.

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup coconut water.
  • 1/2 cup plain greek yogurt.
  • 3 tablespoons almond butter.
  • 1 scoop whey protein powder.
  • 1 tablespoon hulled hemp seeds.
  • 1 frozen banana.
  • 1 cup ice.

Steps:

  1. Just blend everything and enjoy !

Macros:

  • 329 calories, 21g protein, 26g carbs, 5g fiber, 17g fat
